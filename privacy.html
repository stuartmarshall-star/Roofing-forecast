Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Roofing (South East) Limited ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the RSE Forecast application ("the App").

1. About the App

The RSE Forecast App is a private, internal business tool used exclusively by Roofing (South East) Limited and its authorised accountant. It is not available to the general public and is not distributed commercially.

2. Data We Access

The App connects to QuickBooks Online via the Intuit API to read financial data including invoices, bills, and payments. This data is used solely for internal cash flow forecasting and business management purposes.

We access the following QuickBooks data:

  • Sales invoices and customer information
  • Bills and supplier payments
  • Project and customer references

3. How We Use Data

Data accessed from QuickBooks Online is used exclusively to:

  • Populate internal cash flow forecasts
  • Monitor job profitability and billing performance
  • Support internal financial planning

We do not sell, share, or disclose this data to any third parties.

4. Data Storage

Financial data is stored securely in a private Supabase database accessible only to authorised users of Roofing (South East) Limited. Access tokens for QuickBooks Online are stored securely and are never exposed publicly.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as necessary for business forecasting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

As this is a private internal application, data subjects are limited to employees and contractors of Roofing (South East) Limited. Any data access, correction, or deletion requests can be made by contacting stuart.marshall@roofingse.co.uk.
